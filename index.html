Who i am

My name is Winifred Imade Ogbeiwi, and I am a Frontend Developer with a unique background in law and a passion for technology. Based in Warsaw, Poland, I am dedicated to creating impactful solutions and empowering others in the tech ecosystem.

I transitioned from a career in law to software development, earning a scholarship from the Women Techsters Fellowship and securing my first internship within six months. Since then, I have worked on impactful applications like **Firma**, an online marketplace connecting clients with lawyers, while earning certifications such as the Meta Front-End Developer Professional Certificate.

In addition, I started Winning with Wynny, a platform where I mentor and support students in their tech journey, helping them navigate programming challenges and build strong foundations.

Beyond my technical achievements, I am deeply involved in the tech community. I have hosted STEM outreach events, represented Roothub at Rwazi Fest, and served as an alumni speaker for the Women Techsters Fellowship.

In 2024, I was honored to join the Schneider Electric and European Women Mentorship Programs, where I was selected among 110 women from a pool of 1,500 applicants.
